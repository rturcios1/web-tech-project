ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Rosalia. Both of my parents are from El Salvador, so I visit every summer. Despite what you hear on the news, El Salvador is actually a lovely country and I wanted to share that with the world. On this blog you will find guides for visiting different parts of the country and lists of my top 5 restaurant and activity reccomendations.

FAVORITE SPOT

At the moment the destination I recommend the most is La Union. Most tourists like to stay in La Libertad and Suchitoto. La Union offers a similar experience as these two cities but can give you a more authentic experience because it is not over run by foreigners.
